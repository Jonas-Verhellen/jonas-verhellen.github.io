In Short

Silas Therapeutics aims to become a company dedicated to leveraging the power of computation in drug discovery, to create exceptionally high-quality, potent, and selectively targeted enzyme inhibitors. Our approach is agnostic to the disease and focuses on exploiting the substrate selectivity of enzymes, while harnessing cutting-edge computational techniques. With a potential market value of over $75 billion, Silas Therapeutics is well-positioned to revitalize a classic section of the pharmaceutical landscape.
